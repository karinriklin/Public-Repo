ROC Curve — Receiver Operating Characteristic
The curve plots every possible threshold at once. The red dot is your current threshold. Moving it up-left means catching more spam (higher recall) but also flagging more legit mail (higher FPR). A perfect model would hug the top-left corner. The diagonal line = random guessing. AUC summarises the whole curve in one number — closer to 1.0 is better; 0.5 = pure chance.
